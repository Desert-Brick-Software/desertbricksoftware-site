Privacy Policy Place Alerts (Location-Based Reminders) Last Updated: March 17, 2026 Overview Place Alerts (“the App”) is operated by Desert Brick Software LLC (“Company”, “we”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. The App is designed to be local-first and privacy-focused, meaning your data stays on your device unless you choose to share it. Information We Do Not Collect The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers. Specifically, we do not collect: Names, email addresses, or account information Location history stored on external servers Analytics or tracking data Usage profiling or behavioral tracking All reminder data and locations are stored locally on your device only. Information Stored Locally on Your Device The App stores the following information locally on your device: Saved places and associated notes Geofence settings (such as radius and location coordinates) App preferences and settings This data is not accessible to us and is not transmitted externally. Location Data The App uses your device’s location services to determine when you enter a saved area and trigger reminders. Location data is processed on-device only The App does not store or transmit your location data externally The App does not track your continuous location history Diagnostic Logs (Debugging Information) The App may generate diagnostic logs stored locally on your device to help troubleshoot issues. These logs may include technical information such as: App events and state changes Timestamps Approximate or precise location data related to geofence events Important: Logs are stored locally only and are not transmitted automatically We do not have access to these logs You may choose to share logs manually if you contact support If you choose to share logs, they will be used only to investigate and resolve issues. Third-Party Services The App may rely on system or platform services provided by third parties, including: Google Play Services (location services and geofencing) Google Play Billing (if applicable for purchases) These services are governed by their own privacy policies. The App does not currently use third-party analytics or crash reporting tools. Data Access and Control Because all data is stored locally: You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing app storage We cannot access, retrieve, or recover your data Your Privacy Rights Because the App does not collect or store personal data on external servers, most requests related to access, deletion, or correction of personal data can be fulfilled directly on your device. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
